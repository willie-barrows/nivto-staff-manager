## Step 8: Track & Optimize

### Week 1 Check:
After 7 days, go to Page Insights:
- [ ] How many page visits?
- [ ] How many website clicks?
- [ ] Which posts got most engagement?
- [ ] Ad performance: Cost per click? CTR%?

### Adjust Based on Results:

**If ads are working well (CTR > 1%):**
- Increase budget to R75-100/day
- Create 2nd campaign targeting different audience
- Try different ad images/copy

**If ads are expensive (CPC > R5):**
- Narrow audience (target specific industries)
- Improve ad image (more eye-catching)
- Change headline to more compelling offer
- Add urgency: "Limited time offer"

**If posts aren't getting engagement:**
- Post at different times (test 8am, 12pm, 6pm)
- Use more images/videos
- Ask questions to encourage comments
- Run post promotions (R20 boost high-performing posts)

## 🎯 Pro Tips

1. **Post Timing:** Test different times
   - 8-9am (before work starts)
   - 12-1pm (lunch break)
   - 6-7pm (after work)
   - Sunday evenings (planning for week ahead)

2. **Video Content:** Even simple phone videos work!
   - 30-second demo of mobile app
   - "How to set up payroll in 60 seconds"
   - Customer testimonial videos

3. **Urgency Works:** Add deadlines
   - "First 50 customers get..."
   - "This week only..."
   - "Limited time offer..."

4. **Social Proof:** Share milestones
   - "10 businesses now using NIVTO!"
   - "Just helped a customer save 15 hours/month"
   - Screenshot positive messages (with permission)

5. **Answer Common Objections:**
   - "Is it complicated?" → "Set up in 10 minutes"
   - "What if I have questions?" → "Free support included"
   - "Can I cancel anytime?" → "Yes, no lock-in contracts"

---

## ❌ Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Don't over-post:** Max 2-3 posts per day
2. **Don't ignore comments:** Respond within 2 hours
3. **Don't spam groups:** Be helpful, not salesy
4. **Don't give up week 1:** Takes 2-3 weeks to gain traction
5. **Don't run ads without testing:** Start small (R50/day)
6. **Don't forget call to action:** Every post needs clear CTA
7. **Don't copy competitors exactly:** Make it your own voice
