## 🚨 COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. **❌ Posting too much:** Max 2-3 posts/day
   - ✅ Quality over quantity

2. **❌ Ignoring comments:** Slow responses kill engagement
   - ✅ Respond within 2 hours

3. **❌ Running ads without testing:** Wasting money
   - ✅ Start small (R50/day), scale what works

4. **❌ Generic content:** Boring, doesn't engage
   - ✅ Address specific problems, use examples

5. **❌ No clear CTA:** People don't know what to do
   - ✅ Every post: "Download now: [link]"

6. **❌ Giving up too soon:** Takes time to build traction
   - ✅ Commit to 30 days minimum

7. **❌ Not tracking metrics:** Flying blind
   - ✅ Check Ads Manager daily
